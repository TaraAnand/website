Apply to CORE's Startup Internship Program

What is SIP?

The Startup Internship Program (SIP), a program that CORE runs jointly with the Center for Career Education (CCE), connects students with internship opportunities at young, fast-moving startups in Manhattan.
Are you a non-technical student interested in breaking into the world of startups? SIP is your opportunity to do that right here in Manhattan. At CORE, we believe liberal arts students can contribute a lot to any startup's growth. SIP is your opportunity to put your acumen to work in a fast-moving, entrpreneurial work environment.

What do you do as part of SIP?

The SIP program is a combination of internship experience at a startup, peer connections with other SIP interns and professional career development and support with a career counselor. Program features include the following: 

  • Pre-program training that includes program overview, professional skills workshops, panel discussion from startup professionals
  • Twelve-week internship for 10-20 hours per week during the spring semester
  • Networking with other interns to discuss and debrief on internship experiences
  • One-on-one career counseling appointments throughout the twelve weeks to support and enhance the internship experience
  • End-of-program reflection session where students meet to acknowledge achievements made through the program 
Most of all, SIP is not a normal internship program. CORE throws our weight behind ensuring you will experience and enjoy all that startup culture has to offer, and will provide our own mentoring and CORE buddies.

How do I apply?

All SIP employers and positions are listed on Handshake. You may apply to up to three (3) positions of interest in the SIP program.
